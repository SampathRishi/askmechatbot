Today, we recognized and celebrated the retirement of Raul Garcia after 23 years of dedicated service to Cameron County.

Throughout his career, Mr. Garcia played a vital role in improving the quality of life for residents living in colonias. Among his many accomplishments, he helped oversee the completion of 53 colonia improvement projects totaling approximately $65 million in investments that have made a lasting impact on our communities.

While Mr. Garcia will be greatly mis...sed, his passion, leadership, and commitment to public service will continue to be reflected in the work of the Planning and Development Department for years to come.

Thank you, Raul, for your 23 years of service and dedication to Cameron County. We wish you all the best in your retirement.
